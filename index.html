Our Values

We're more than just a candle company.

Sustainability

Passionate about caring for this planet, we upcycle baby food jars to use as containers for our candles. All ingredients are 100% natural and sourced locally.

Health

We care about your mental and physical well-being. BeeFresh candles contain beeswax with purifying properties that cleanse the surrounding air, freeing it from toxic pollutants, dust, and dirt.

Community

We strive to foster a positive experience for all our users and give back to our community! 100% of all profits will be going towards non-profit organizations.
